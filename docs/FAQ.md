# FAQ

## What does ZPE-Image encode today?

It encodes a bounded set of sparse-stroke figures, plus a narrower route for hole-bearing sparse figures.

## Does it encode natural photos or broad textured imagery?

No. Broad natural-image coverage is explicitly out of scope.

## Is the hole-bearing route the default route?

No. It is narrower than the main sparse-stroke route and stays non-default.

## Does the repo claim broader cross-product admission?

No. The claim surface is limited to this repo's two bounded scopes.
